Hello! I am an Associate Professor at the School of Robotics, Beijing Union University, Beijing, China (since Mar. 2025). My research focuses on state-of-the-art multi-agent cooperative estimation, optimization, and control methods applied to autonomous agents and sensor networks.

Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Associate in the Artificial Intelligence Lab at Vrije Universiteit Brussel (2021-2022), working with [Prof. Ann Nowé](https://ai.vub.ac.be/team/ann-nowe) to explore and develop safe and explainable (Multi-agent) reinforcement learning (RL). Prior to that, I served as a Lecturer at Beijing Union University from 2017 to 2025.

I received my Ph.D. degree in Control Science and Engineering under the supervision of Prof. Jie Chen in 2017, and my B.Eng. degree in Electrical Engineering and Automation in 2011, both from the [Beijing Institute of Technology](https://english.bit.edu.cn/), Beijing, China. During my Ph.D., I was a visiting scholar at Loughborough University, UK (2014-2015), working with Prof. Shuang-Hua Yang.

My research interests mainly focus on:
* Multi-agent cooperative robust adaptive estimation, inference, and optimization under unknown system or measurement statistics.
* Risk-sensitive multi-agent robust control and RL under uncertainties.
* Cooperative indoor localization and target tracking under uncertainties (e.g., IMU/UWB sensor fusion, connected vehicles).
* Intelligent optimization and decision-making for complex systems.
